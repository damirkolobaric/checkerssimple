🎮 HOW TO PLAY

Controls:
  • TAP red piece to select it
  • TAP destination to move
  • PAN to rotate camera
  • PINCH to zoom

Buttons:
  • Reset - New game
  • AI Difficulty - Easy/Medium/Hard
  • Sound - Toggle sound effects
  • Turn Label - Shows current player

Rules:
  • Red moves first
  • AI (Black) responds automatically
  • Move diagonally
  • Jump to capture
  • Reach end to become King
